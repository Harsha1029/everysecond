About Us

The mission behind everysecond.live

At everysecond, we believe that data has the power to change perspective. Our platform provides a real-time window into the massive, unfolding scale of human activity and natural phenomena on our planet.

Our Vision

In a world of static headlines, we aim to provide a "live" sense of urgency and awareness. Whether it's the rate of reforestation or the volume of digital messages sent, seeing the numbers tick up in real-time makes the abstract feel immediate.

The Data

Our statistics are calculated using reliable annual benchmarks from global organizations including the WHO, UN, and various scientific institutes. We divide these annual figures into second-by-second increments to project a live estimate of global activity.
